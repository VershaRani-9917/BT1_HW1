Records

Virat is the first player in ICC cricket history to win all 3 ICC awards in a single year- ICC , ODI player of the year, ICC Test player of the year and ICC Player of the year award in 2018. He is rated as one of the best batsmen in the world. He plays right handed-batting. He is considered as one of the greatest batsmen of all time. In ODIs, he boasts an average of 65.5 runs when batting second and 51.7 runs when batting first.
